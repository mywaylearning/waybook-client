There’s magic in a goal.

Goals should correspond to positive milestones in your life, or habits you want to form. You can categorize your goals with #hashtags, and make them visual by associating them with an image. Live with intention.

Forming the right habits are critical for success.

What you repeatedly do, ultimately defines the person you become. You can set goals to form new habits. Habits recur in the following cycle:

cue → routine → reward

Habits are trigged by CUES such as time, place, another activity, a feeling, a sound, a smell, etc.. The ROUTINE is the habit itself. The REWARD is the beneficial consequence of the routine. The best rewards are a natural consequence.

It may take a hundreds of times through this cycle for the routine to become a natural habit.
