There’s magic in a goal.

Goals should correspond to positive milestones in your life, or habits you want to form. You can categorize your goals with #hashtags, and make them visual by associating them with an image. Live with intention.

Forming the right habits are critical for success.

What you repeatedly do, ultimately defines the person you become.

Some goals are recurring, and are actually a goal to form a new habit. Habits recur in the following cycle:

cue → routine → reward

Cue: Habits are routines triggered by cues. Those cues can be a time, a place, another activity, a feeling, a sound, a smell, or something else. For example you hear the phone ring.

Routine: This is the habit itself. For example, you pick up the phone and say hello.

Rewards: The reward is the beneficial consequence of the routine. The best rewards are a natural consequence. For example, your reward is that your curiosity is satisfied, or you connect with another human.

It may take a hundreds of times through this cycle for the routine to become a natural habit.
