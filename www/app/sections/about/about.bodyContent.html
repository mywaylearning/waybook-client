Core belief
Everyone has genius.
Objective
Discover your genius, and leverage it to create a healthy, happy, and meaningful life.
Core Design Principles
  1. You own your future. This app was designed for you lifelong.
  2. The waybook helps you look at the big picture. Every decision, every step you make, is within a bigger context.
  3. The waybook will be accessible to all.
Why
Using the waybook you’ll understand yourself better, develop a plan for your life, build a reasonable framework for decision making, and be more thoughtful about those whom you engage for support. You’ll be more motivated, have more confidence, and ultimately be more successful. More at http://about.way.me.
